About Hostel

Vivekanand hostel

PDPM IIITDM is a residential institute and it is compulsory for all students to stay inside-campus. Vivekanand hostel endeavour to make home away from home;providing a clean, congenial and dynamic environment to the inmates to assist in their cocurricular as well as extracurricular growth besides assured academics with discipline,which are the necessary tools to face real life situations. Housing close to 450 students from across different programmes and departments, the diversity of the hostel across region is something to be proud about.The sole motto behind the workforce of hostel maintenance is to keep everything running. Study Room,Canteen, TV Room are some of the facilities which are available in our hostel.
